Our Founder

Visionary leadership in healthcare innovation

Dr Vas Metupalle

Founder & CEO, 1Life Healthcare

Dr Vas Metupalle is a healthcare visionary and entrepreneur with extensive experience in healthcare consulting, digital transformation, and health technology innovation across Asia-Pacific and the Middle East.

With a deep understanding of the intersection between technology and healthcare delivery, Dr Metupalle founded 1Life Healthcare to bridge the gap between traditional healthcare systems and modern digital solutions. His vision encompasses a future where AI, IoT, and data-driven platforms transform how care is delivered, managed, and experienced by patients worldwide.

Based across Singapore and Dubai, Dr Metupalle leads strategic partnerships with leading technology companies and healthcare organizations, including collaborations with AJMS Global, AInsurTek, and Trivitron Digital to create comprehensive healthcare ecosystems.

Under his leadership, 1Life Healthcare has become a trusted partner for healthcare organizations seeking to navigate the complexities of digital transformation while maintaining a patient-centric approach.
